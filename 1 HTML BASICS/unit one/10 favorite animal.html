Giraffes

A Giraffe is a mammal just like us it is the tallest land animal because of its long legs and long neck. Giraffes are very unique which makes them easy to spot. along with their long legs and neck they also have spotted fur. Even though Giraffes are big animals they are actually prey to a lot of other animals, like lions, and leapords.
