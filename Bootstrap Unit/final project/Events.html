The National Women's Soccer Team Camp Details

The schedleue

This camp is a 3 day long camp spaning from Friday through Sunday. A lot of things will happen at this soccer camp: first you will have the most fun you have ever had, second you will be playing your sport which you love, and third you will be able to meet our guest speakers.

    This is just a quick overview of what a day at this camp will look like.

  • Friday- First meetings with team and first two trainings with a guest coach.
  • Saturday - Conference where I have selected 6 speakers involved with the National Women's soccer Team to come and speak to you guys all about how to make it to the next level.
  • Sunday-Church, scrimmages, awards ceremony, and pickup.
