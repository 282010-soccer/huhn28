The National United States Soccer Camp

Our Mission

Our Mission is to help girls make the next step in bettering themselves not just on the field but in life too. We want to help girls make it to the next level by giving them oppourtunites that will help them with that. Also we are giving the girls a chance to hear how people who have been on the National Women's Soccer team got to that level.

Our Vision

We envision one day a lot more girls will have the passion for a sport and do their best in every game they play. We want more gils to try for things like doing what is best for them to succeed.

Our Organazation

We are an organazation that prides on helping girls make the next step in their soccer carrers. We invite all girls who have a strong passion for soccer and want to pursue a career in soccer. We want girls who want to go to college for soccer and always put in their most effort in order to succeed. Our goals of this camp is to give girls the oppurtuniity to make friends and to hear how players who made it to the professional level had to go through to make it to that stage in their soccer carrers.
