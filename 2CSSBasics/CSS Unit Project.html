How I got Into Baking!

I have a lot of hobbys but my all time favorite one is to bake. I love to bake desserts for my family. I have a big sweet tooth so I love to eat yummy deserts. My love for baking sprung up when I went over to my neighbors house. My family is very good friends with our neighbors so during covid when I couldn't go to school my neighbor would invite my sister and I over to have lunch with her(she is a very good cook). All of her meals were homeade and delicious. On my birthday she made me coffee cake and it was so good, I asked for the recipe and made it. Thats when my love for baking came about.
